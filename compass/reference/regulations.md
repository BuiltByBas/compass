# Regulations Reference

A library entry Compass consults by name when a regulation is flagged. Plain language, not
legal advice. For each: what it is, who it applies to, and what Compass flags. Compass
names the regulation in WHY and in the draft, and notes that compliant handling is scoped at the
consultation. It never claims to certify compliance.

## Data and privacy

- GDPR (EU): privacy law for EU residents' personal data. Applies to any site with EU users.
  Flag: consent, data-subject rights, lawful handling.
- CCPA / CPRA (California): California consumer privacy. Applies to sites serving California
  users above thresholds. Flag: privacy policy, opt-out of sale/share, data rights.
- PCI-DSS: card-payment security standard. Applies to anyone taking card payments. Flag: use a
  compliant processor, never store raw card data.
- HIPAA / HITECH: protects health information. Applies to healthcare and anyone handling PHI.
  Flag: PHI security, business-associate agreements, secure portals.
- COPPA: protects children under 13 online. Applies to kids' products, games, edtech. Flag:
  verifiable parental consent, no behavioral ads to children.
- FERPA: protects student education records. Applies to schools and edtech serving institutions.
  Flag: access controls, consent for disclosure.
- GLBA: protects consumer financial data. Applies to financial firms, lenders, advisors, and
  auto dealers handling financing. Flag: safeguards rule, privacy notices.
- BIPA (Illinois): biometric privacy. Applies to anything capturing face or fingerprint data.
  Flag: explicit consent before any biometric capture.

## Accessibility

- ADA / WCAG / Section 508: accessibility of digital content. Applies to all public sites,
  strictly for government, healthcare, and education. Flag: build to WCAG, a BuiltByBas strength.

## Marketing and communications

- CAN-SPAM / CASL: email-marketing rules (US and Canada). Applies to any email-marketing
  feature. Flag: working unsubscribe, honest sender identity, consent (CASL is stricter).
- TCPA: governs automated calls and texts. Applies to SMS or voice reminders and marketing.
  Flag: prior express consent, easy opt-out.

## Industry-specific

- SEC / FINRA: securities and advisor advertising and recordkeeping. Applies to registered
  investment advisors and broker-dealers. Flag: advertising rules, recordkeeping.
- FCRA: fair credit reporting. Applies to tenant or employment screening. Flag: consent,
  adverse-action notices.
- Fair Housing Act: bars discriminatory housing advertising. Applies to real estate and
  property management. Flag: no discriminatory copy or ad targeting.
- ITAR / EAR: export controls on defense and technical data. Applies to defense, aerospace, and
  some manufacturing. Flag: access controls, no exposure of controlled data.
- FTC advertising and health claims: bars deceptive claims. Applies to supplements, pharma,
  testimonials, and endorsements. Flag: substantiate claims, disclose endorsements.
- RESPA: real-estate settlement procedures. Applies to mortgage, title, and closing services.
  Flag: required disclosures, no kickbacks for referrals.
- Reg Z (Truth in Lending): consumer-credit disclosure. Applies to lenders and dealers offering
  financing. Flag: clear APR and loan-term disclosure.
- SOC 2: a security and controls audit framework (not a law). Applies to SaaS handling customer
  data. Flag: expected by enterprise buyers; design with it in mind and scope at consult.
